Biriyani

Description

Biriyani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that brings together spices, meat, and basmati rice in a beautiful harmony. Though it takes time to prepare, it's a truly rewarding dish.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken or Mutton
  • Yogurt
  • Onion
  • Spices
  • Tomato
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat
  2. Cook the rice
  3. Fry the onions
  4. Layer the rice and meat
  5. Cook on low heat (dum)
  6. Let it rest before serving
