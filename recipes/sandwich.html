Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is a quick and satisfying meal made by placing fillings between slices of bread. It's perfect for lunch, picnics, or a light dinner.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ham or Turkey
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Toast or prepare the bread
  2. Spread butter and mayonnaise
  3. Add meat and cheese
  4. Add lettuce and tomato
  5. Close the sandwich
  6. Cut and serve
